DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM (DPA)

Governing Regulations: GDPR (EU 2016/679) and UK Data Protection Act 2018
Processor: NorthBridge Advisory Group (data processor)
Controller: Client organization (data controller)

PERSONAL DATA CATEGORIES PROCESSED
- Employee personal data: Names, IDs, emails, phone numbers
- Management information: Performance ratings, salary bands (anonymized), reporting structure
- Contact information: Business email, office location, department affiliation
- System access logs: Login IDs, IP addresses, timestamp records
- Project assignment data: Project codes, role assignments, time allocations

SECURITY CONTROLS IMPLEMENTED
Encryption at Rest: AES-256 standard
Encryption in Transit: TLS 1.2 minimum
Access Control: Role-based access control (RBAC)
Multi-factor Authentication: Required for all user access
Audit Logging: Complete audit trail maintained
Backup Strategy: Daily encrypted backups with quarterly restoration testing

PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS
- Process data only on documented Controller instructions
- Ensure staff confidentiality obligations in writing
- Approve sub-processors in writing (list maintained)
- Restrict data access to need-to-know personnel
- Respond to data subject requests within 30 days
- Provide annual Data Processing Impact Assessment (DPIA)
- Notify Controller of breach within 24 hours of discovery
- Delete/return all data within 30 days of engagement termination

DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS SUPPORT
Right of Access: Copy of personal data within 5 business days
Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data within 3 business days
Right to Erasure: Delete data upon request within 10 days
Right to Data Portability: Export in machine-readable format within 5 days
Right to Object: Honor objections within 10 business days

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
Transfer Mechanism: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
Data Import Country: United States
Legal Framework: EU-US Data Privacy Framework
Processing Locations: US-East (primary), US-West (backup)

APPROVED SUB-PROCESSORS
- Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure
- Microsoft Azure: Backup systems provider
- Okta: Identity and access management

AUDIT RIGHTS AND COMPLIANCE
Controller Audit Rights: Permitted with 15 days notice
Certification: SOC 2 Type II (annual audit)
Compliance Certification: GDPR compliance as of effective date
Incident Reporting: Security incidents reported within 24 hours
