BACKGROUND / RECITALS
==================================================================

Plaintiff filed a complaint on November 2, 2024, in the United States District Court
for the Northern District of Georgia, Case No. 1:24-cv-04512-AT, asserting claims for:

  (1) Wrongful termination in violation of the Americans with Disabilities Act
      (42 U.S.C. § 12101 et seq.) related to Plaintiff's diagnosis of Type 2
      diabetes requiring accommodation;
  (2) Retaliation under the ADA following Plaintiff's August 2024 EEOC charge;
  (3) Failure to accommodate (interactive process claim);
  (4) State law claim: intentional infliction of emotional distress (Georgia).

Defendant denies all allegations and admits no liability whatsoever.

The parties, having engaged in good-faith arm's-length negotiations facilitated
by mediator Judge (Ret.) Patricia Owens on January 30, 2026, agree to settle fully
and finally on the terms set forth herein.
