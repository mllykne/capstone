SECTION 4: DATA SECURITY & CONFIDENTIALITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Employees must maintain strict confidentiality regarding:
- All client information including names, contracts, financial data, strategies
- Personal data of fellow employees (SSNs, salaries, performance ratings, home addresses)
- Firm financial data including revenue, partner compensation, pipeline information
- Proprietary methodologies, frameworks, and intellectual property

Violations may result in immediate termination and/or legal action.

CLIENT DATA PROTECTION
Under various client contracts, Meridian staff may have access to:
- Patient health records (PHI) governed by HIPAA
- Financial records governed by various banking regulations
- Personal data of client employees governed by state privacy laws (CCPA, CPRA, etc.)

All such data must be handled according to the relevant contractual requirements and applicable law. Contact the General Counsel (s.mitchell@meridian-consulting.com) with any data privacy questions.

SECTION 5: COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Compensation information is strictly confidential. Employees may not discuss their own or others' compensation with colleagues outside of the HR or Finance functions.

Standard Benefits:
- Medical: BCBS options (Platinum, Silver, Bronze)
- Dental: Delta Dental
- Vision: VSP
- 401(k): 4% employer match, vesting 3 years
- Life: 1-3x salary depending on level
- Short/Long Term Disability: Company-paid

SECTION 8: TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All business travel must be booked through Concur Travel.
Meal limits: Breakfast $30, Lunch $50, Dinner $100, Client dinner $125/person.
Hotel: Up to $350/night in Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, Chicago, Boston, DC).
All receipts required for expenses > $25.
Submit via Concur within 30 days of expense date.
