1. ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All Meridian employees and contractors must use firm technology resources (laptops, phones, email, VPN, cloud services) exclusively for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not compromise firm security or productivity.

Prohibited activities:
- Installing unauthorized software on firm devices
- Using personal cloud storage (personal Dropbox, Google Drive) for client data
- Connecting to public Wi-Fi without activating the Meridian VPN (Cisco AnyConnect)
- Sharing credentials with any other party including colleagues
- Accessing client systems with personal devices unless approved by IT

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. DATA CLASSIFICATION POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Level 1 — Public: Marketing materials, published whitepapers, public website content
Level 2 — Internal: Internal memos, staff announcements, training materials
Level 3 — Confidential: Client data, financial results, HR records, partner comp
Level 4 — Restricted: Passwords, security keys, PII containing SSN/DOB, BAA-protected PHI

All Level 3 and 4 data must be stored in approved SharePoint libraries or encrypted at rest.
Sharing Level 3/4 data via email requires encryption (use Outlook S/MIME or Virtru).
