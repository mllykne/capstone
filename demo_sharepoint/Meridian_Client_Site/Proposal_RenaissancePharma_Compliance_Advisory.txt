EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
RenaissancePharma Inc. ("Client") faces increasing regulatory scrutiny following the FDA's February 2026 Warning Letter (WL-2026-023) related to data integrity gaps in clinical trial management. Meridian proposes a 9-month engagement to remediate compliance deficiencies, establish a robust data governance framework, and prepare the organization for re-inspection.

PROPOSED APPROACH
Workstream 1: FDA Readiness Assessment
- Review all 21 CFR Part 11 electronic records compliance gaps
- Full audit of clinical trial data systems (Veeva Vault, Oracle Clinical)
- Remediation roadmap with prioritized action items

Workstream 2: Data Governance Framework
- Establish Data Stewardship Council
- Implement data classification policy across R&D data (applies to ~4.2M patient records)
- Deploy automated PHI detection across document repositories

Workstream 3: Training & Culture Change
- Compliance awareness training for 1,200 employees
- GCP refresher training for clinical operations team (340 staff)
